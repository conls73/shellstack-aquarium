How it works

Set it up once. Then just glance at your desktop.

1

Install the app

Download for Windows. It runs as a transparent overlay on your desktop.

2

Install the Chrome extension

The extension watches your Gmail and Slack tabs and sends unread counts to the app.

3

Watch your inbox swim

Fish appear when messages are unread, grow as they age, and vanish when you reply.
